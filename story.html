The Seven Deadly Sins:
Grand Cross

In the old days when people
still lived side by side with other races,

many respected and feared the Holy Knights,
possessing tremendous power.

They were protectors
Kingdom of Liones.

Suspecting the Holy Knights of betrayal,
Princess Elizabeth goes on a journey
to find those who can stop the knights.

She seeks traitors to the kingdom
and enemies of the Holy Knights,
[The Seven Deadly Sins].

Eventually Elizabeth meets young man Meliodas -
this is how a great adventure begins,
which will turn the fate of the world.
