DEATHMATCH

Deathmatch/Raids are battles against a powerful demon that you can participate in by yourself or with an ally in real-time.You can invite a friend or be matched up with a random player. Each player will have 3 units plus a substitute. During your turn, both will players act at the same time. The order of actions is determined by the speed at which they are selected.
