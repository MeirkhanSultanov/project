Seven Deadly Sins: Grand Cross is a free-to-play, turn-based mobile game developed by Netmarble based off of the popular anime, Seven Deadly Sins. It first launched in Japan and Korea before making its global launch in 2020. It follows the story of the anime very closely, even using cutscenes from the show itself.
